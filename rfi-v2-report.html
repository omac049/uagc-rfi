Executive Summary & Methodology

Purpose & Scope

The Request for Information (RFI) form is UAGC's primary lead-generation tool‚Äîthe critical first touchpoint where prospective students express interest in our programs. Every submission represents a potential enrollment, making form optimization directly tied to revenue.

This analysis examines: How four leading online universities structure their RFI forms to maximize completion rates, data quality, and lead conversion.

Methodology

Research Approach

Competitive Set Selection: We analyzed four universities based on:

  • Large online enrollment (50,000+ students)
  • Direct competitors in adult/online education market
  • Established digital marketing presence
  • Known for lead generation excellence
